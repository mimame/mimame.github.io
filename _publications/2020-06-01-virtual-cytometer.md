Single-Cell Virtual Cytometer is a tool designed to simplify the exploration of multimodal single-cell datasets, such as CITE-seq. It enables researchers to visualize transcriptomic and immunophenotypic data simultaneously in a flow cytometry-like interface, facilitating the identification of cell populations and functional states.
